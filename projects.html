Advertisement Report Dashboard

This is a project I developed alongside another intern during the summer of 2015 at Idmloco. This application's primary function was to automatically generate reports that would typically take another employee 5-10 hours per week to complete. The application is currently hosted and can be accessed here, however both Google Adwords and Facebook Marketing accounts with advertisement data are needed for the application to gather data from. My main portion of the work was all of the Django templating, url processing, and portions of the data acquisition. Specific files showcasing my contributions are (repository):

  • report/views.py
  • report/templates/report/grandview.html
  • report/templates/report/select.html
  • report/urls.py
  • adwords/urls.py
